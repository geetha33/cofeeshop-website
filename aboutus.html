Campus Coffee is one of the premier specialty coffee shop in the country. Campus Coffee has been roasting and blending specialty coffee since 1992 where we grew from a small roaster retailer operation into one of the largest specialty roasters in the Midwest. Over the years the one constant has been our passion for the specialty coffee industry and our unbending commitment to freshly roasting the beans to order.

Caruso's Coffee buys only the finest Arabica coffees from the best growing regions in the world. These include numerous Fair Trade Organic certified selections. All our coffee is roasted to order and either delivered or shipped within 24 hours of roasting. We currently ship product throughout the United States and overseas. Any coffee shipping via UPS or common carrier is back flushed with nitrogen prior to being vacuum sealed. This ensures your coffee is as fresh as the day it was roasted when it arrives at your door.
